About Me



I'm currently a Firm-wide Design Technology Specialist at HOK, conducting research and development for AR/VR and adjacent emerging technologies. My passion is to build novel interactive applications and their corresponding UI/UX. I spend most of my time in Unity solving human centered design problems, and use Javascript and C# to help me solve them. Before my life in goggles, I worked on several large scale commercial architecture projects as a BIM Lead/Design Professional. Most notably, I lead BIM strategy and coordination on the new GroupM Global Headquarters, in World Trade Center 3. I earned my Masters of Architecture from Pratt Institute in Brooklyn, NY. where I emphasized my studies in Design Technology. I'm also currently taking a Fullstack Web Development certificate at Columbia University, with the goal of building scalable end to end applications. Outside of work I always have fun side projects going, mostly tinkering with various emerging technologies and consulting for a variety of companies. I'm a hardworking and passionate hybrid designer who loves to work with a team to push what is possible.
